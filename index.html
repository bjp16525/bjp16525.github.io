The College Football Playoff

Having lost our most recent in conference game to Auburn, UGA has been set back a little from their goal of reaching the College Football Playoff. However, given that we have already qualified for the SEC Championship, if we are able to defeat Kentucky and Georgia Tech in our final two regular season games, we will enter the SEC championship with only one loss. This means that if we are able to beat the SEC West's best team and become conference champions, we can fully expect to make the playoff. This would be UGA's first appearance in the playoff since the NCAA first introduced it in 2014.
